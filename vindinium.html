What is vindinium?

Vindinium is a game in which people can create their very own unique AI (Artificial intellegence) bot, and compete with other users. The goal in vindinium is to make sure your bot collects the most amount of gold in the given amount of turns. You can get gold by capturing free mines, by capturing already owned mines, or by killing another person. Also in vindinium, when your bot is at low health it can go to a tavern so that it can heal. Each person can use different strategies to win by coding there bot differently then others. You can customize your bot by choosing when your bot should attack someone, if your bot should only focus on mines, etc. There is an endless amount of things you can do with your bot!
